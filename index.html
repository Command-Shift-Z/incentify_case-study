Incentify

Sometimes people struggle with completing their tasks and goals because they are disorganized with establishing good habits and have priorities that prevent them from maintaining their other tasks.

Incentify helps users to establish tasks, goals, and habits they want to complete in a timely and organized manner. Also, users can connect with social groups to challenge themselves at completing their tasks.

Before User Interviews

In the beginning, we made assumptions about the persona, which is a college student who’s unmotivated to go to class and complete their class work. We wanted to learn more about the user so we created a Google Form to gather data from our peers and others.

From the results we gathered on the Google Form survey, we wanted to learn more about user’s motivations to completing tasks and goals and use the data to create the questions to help us find issues and solutions to their problems.

What Keeps Users Motivated?

After interviewing six people within the age ranges of 19–35, we learned the similarities and differences users had to motivate them to complete their goals and tasks. Most people are motivated to complete their task based on personal rewards and avoid negative issues when they don’t complete them. Also, users have to deal with many obstacles to complete their tasks, which are usually the lack of time and distractions from other tasks.
